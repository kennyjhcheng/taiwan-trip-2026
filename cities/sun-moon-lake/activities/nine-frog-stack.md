# Nine Frog Stack (九蛙疊像)

> #sightseeing · **✨ Worth It** · 💰 NT$0 · ⬜ Pending

Famous lakeside sculpture of nine stone frogs stacked in a column on a rock at the water's edge. A Sun Moon Lake icon — the lake's water level determines how many frogs are visible. Quick photo stop, ~10–15 min.
