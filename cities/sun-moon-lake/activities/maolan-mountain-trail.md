# Maolan Mountain Tea Plantation Trail (貓囒山步道)

> #activity · #sightseeing · **🔥 Must** · 💰 NT$0 · ⬜ Pending

Trail through the Assam tea plantations that produce Sun Moon Lake Red Tea (日月潭紅茶). The slopes of Maolan Mountain are where the famous tea grows — rows of tea bushes with the lake below. Shorter plantation loop ~1–1.5 hrs; full summit ~2.5 hrs. Do the plantation loop only given the packed day.

Located north of Shuishe — accessible from the cycling route heading toward Wenwu Temple.
