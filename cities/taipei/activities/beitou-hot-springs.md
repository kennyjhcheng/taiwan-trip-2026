# Beitou Hot Springs (北投溫泉)

> #activity · #hotspring · **✨ Worth It** · 💰 NT$150 · ✅ Done

Went to **Beitou Qinghua Hot Spring (北投青磺名湯)** — NT$150 entry + NT$50 towel rental. Communal public sulfur bath, almost entirely elderly local Taiwanese men. Different from the tourist-facing Millennium Hot Spring but authentic and relaxing in its own way.
