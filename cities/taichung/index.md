## 🚌 Getting Around

Taichung has **no MRT** (the Green Line opens later). Use:
- **YouBike 2.0** — great for flat central city routes, NT$10/30 min
- **Taxi / Uber** — cheap, ~NT$150–200 cross-city
- **Bus** — useful but slow; Google Maps routing works well
- **Scooter rental** — if you're comfortable; needed for Xinshe hills

**Taichung → Sun Moon Lake (Apr 28):**
Nantou Bus from Taichung HSR Station (台中高鐵站) — NT$193, ~1 hr. Departs roughly hourly 08:00–18:00.
