# Night School Braised Pork Rice (夜校滷肉飯)

A fan-favorite Taichung braised pork rice joint — the kind of place that attracts long queues from locals who return again and again. The name ("night school") hints at its late-night origins and the fact that it's often open when other places have closed.

## What to Order

- **Braised Pork Rice (滷肉飯)** — the centerpiece; slow-cooked fatty pork belly in soy-based sauce over steamed rice, NT$50–80
- **Braised Egg (滷蛋)** — must add-on, NT$15–25
- **Side dishes** — bamboo shoots, tofu, braised intestine; pick from the display

## Tips

- Check current opening hours — may be evening-only or have limited lunch hours
- Can get crowded; arrive just as they open
- Pair with a simple soup (usually free or very cheap)
- Cash preferred at most Taiwanese braised rice shops
