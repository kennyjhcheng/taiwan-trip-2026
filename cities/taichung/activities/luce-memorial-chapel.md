# Luce Memorial Chapel (路思義教堂)

An iconic modernist chapel on the campus of **Tunghai University**, designed by **I.M. Pei** (architect of the Louvre pyramid) and completed in 1963. The structure is a hyperbolic paraboloid form — two curved concrete shells that taper to a narrow ridge at the top, like hands pressed together in prayer. One of the finest examples of modernist religious architecture in Asia.

## Tips

- **Free to enter**; open daily on the university campus
- Takes 20–30 min to visit; the surrounding campus is beautifully landscaped
- Tunghai University grounds are pleasant for a walk — tree-lined brick paths, older buildings
- Best combined with **Taichung National Theatre** nearby (~15 min taxi)
- Also near **Fengchia Night Market** — works as a pre-dinner stop

## Getting Here

On the Tunghai University campus; Uber/taxi from National Theatre is ~NT$100. In the same Xitun corridor as the National Theatre and Fengchia.
