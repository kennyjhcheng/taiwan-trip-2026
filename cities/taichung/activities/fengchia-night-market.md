# Fengchia Night Market 逢甲夜市

One of Taiwan's biggest night markets, adjacent to Feng Chia University. Monday nights = manageable crowds. Explore the side alleys for the more local stalls.

## Notes (Apr 27)
Evening 6:30–9:30 PM. Had:
- **Woo Tea** — drinks
- **Takoyaki** — octopus balls
- **Chopped braised meat & tofu** — lu wei (滷味) stall
