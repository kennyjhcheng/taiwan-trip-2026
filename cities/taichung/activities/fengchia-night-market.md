# Fengchia Night Market (逢甲夜市)

One of Taiwan's **largest and most popular night markets** — consistently ranked in the top 3 nationally. Centered around Fengchia University, the market sprawls across multiple streets and alleys. Unlike older night markets, Fengchia is constantly innovating: new food trends launch here before spreading to the rest of Taiwan.

## Must-Eat List

- **Grilled Corn (烤玉米)** — brushed with butter and soy, iconic
- **Oyster Vermicelli (蚵仔麵線)** — thick, gelatinous noodle soup with oysters
- **Stinky Tofu (臭豆腐)** — deep-fried, with kimchi and pickled daikon
- **Taiwanese Sausage with Sticky Rice (大腸包小腸)** — the "big intestine wraps small intestine" classic
- **Bubble Tea / Fruit Slushies** — try a local independent vendor rather than a chain
- **Crispy Chicken Cutlet (雞排)** — pan-sized, pounded thin, seasoned with basil and salt

## Tips

- Peak hours: **6 PM – 10 PM**; can be extremely crowded on weekends; Monday is much more manageable
- Wear comfortable shoes — lots of walking on uneven cobbles
- Budget NT$200–400 easily, NT$600+ if you're sampling widely
- Cash preferred at most stalls; some accept LINE Pay
- The main market runs along **Wenhua Road, Fengchia Road, and Fude Road**
- Walk the side alleys for less touristy, more local stalls
