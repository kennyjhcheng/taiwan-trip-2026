# Fantasy Story Green Ray (幻想故事屋 綠光)

A beloved Taichung indie store selling vintage toys, figures, collectibles, retro games, and anime/character merch — a deep rabbit hole for anyone who grew up with Japanese pop culture. Known for its eclectic curation of rare finds and a slightly nostalgic, fantasy atmosphere.

## What to Expect

- Walls lined with vintage Gundam, Sailor Moon, Dragon Ball figures and rare imports
- A mix of new and second-hand collectibles at varying price points
- Staff are enthusiasts — good conversation if you're into the genre

## Tips

- Budget time to browse properly — easy to spend an hour here
- NT$0 to enter; budget whatever your self-control allows
- Confirm hours before heading over (typically open afternoon–evening)
