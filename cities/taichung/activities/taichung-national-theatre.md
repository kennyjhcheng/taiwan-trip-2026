# Taichung National Theatre (臺中國家歌劇院)

One of the most architecturally remarkable buildings in Asia — designed by Pritzker Prize–winning architect **Toyo Ito**. The structure has no flat surfaces or right angles; it's made entirely of curved concrete "sound caves" that create a flowing, organic form. Even if there's no performance, walking around and through the building is worth the trip.

## What to Do

- Walk the exterior — the curved white-concrete façade is stunning from every angle
- Enter the free ground-floor lobby and atrium — open daily
- Check if there are any performances (opera, theatre, dance) — tickets range NT$300–3,000
- The rooftop garden and terrace café are open to the public

## Tips

- Free to enter the public areas; performances are ticketed
- **Best light for photos:** late afternoon (golden hour hits the south facade beautifully)
- The indoor curves and tunnels are as photogenic as the exterior
- Combined well with Fengchia Night Market — they're in the same Xitun district, ~10 min apart
