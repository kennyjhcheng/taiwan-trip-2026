# Zhongshe Tourist Flower Market (中社觀光花市)

A large outdoor flower market in the foothills northeast of Taichung, featuring seasonal flower gardens, vendor stalls, and scenic mountain views. Popular for its sweeping fields of tulips, roses, and seasonal blooms. A tranquil countryside escape from the city.

## What to Expect

- Seasonal flower fields — in late April, expect late-spring blooms (roses, carnations, seasonal varieties)
- Vendors selling cut flowers, potted plants, local produce, and snacks
- Café and picnic area with views of the hills

## Logistics

⚠️ **Out of the way** — located in the Houli/Xinshe foothills, ~50 min drive from central Taichung. Best combined with Xinshey Manor as they're in the same eastern hills area.

- Scooter or taxi required; no direct transit
- Entry is typically free; some flower viewing areas may charge NT$100–150
- Opens daily; busiest on weekends

## Best Combined With

[新社莊園 (Xinshey Manor / Villa)](xinshey-manor.md) — both are in the Xinshe district. Plan a half-day for both.
