# 新社莊園 (Xinshey Manor)

A scenic estate in Taichung's eastern mountain district of Xinshe — known for flower gardens, rolling hills, European-style architecture, and seasonal blooms (cherry blossoms in spring; cosmos flowers in autumn). A popular weekend retreat for Taichung locals.

## What to Expect

- Landscaped flower gardens and estate grounds
- Café/restaurant on-site with views of the hills
- Photography-friendly with plenty of scenic spots

## Logistics

⚠️ **This is out of the way** — Xinshe is ~45 min by taxi/scooter from downtown Taichung, heading into the eastern foothills. A taxi there and back would cost NT$600–900+.

- Best visited as a **half-day excursion** combined with Zhongshe Flower Market (they're in the same area)
- Only realistic by rental scooter or private taxi — no convenient bus
- Entry fee approximately NT$100–200

## When to Go

Flower season (late March–April and October–November) is the peak time; visiting in late April should still have some blooms.
