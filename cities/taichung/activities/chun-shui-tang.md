# Chun Shui Tang Siwei Main Shop (春水堂四維店)

**The birthplace of bubble tea.** Liu Han-Chieh invented tapioca pearl milk tea here in the 1980s — this Siwei Street branch is the original flagship. Coming here is a pilgrimage for any tea lover.

## What to Order

- **Pearl Milk Tea (珍珠奶茶)** — the OG, NT$120. Don't leave without it
- **Iced Brown Sugar Milk Tea** — slightly more complex, caramelized sweetness
- **Braised Pork Rice Set (滷肉飯套餐)** — the kitchen is surprisingly good; great lunch option
- **Taro Ball Dessert** — seasonal but excellent

## Tips

- This is a **sit-down tea house**, not a takeaway stand — enjoy the experience
- Can get busy at lunch; arrive before 12 PM or after 2 PM
- The Siwei shop is the **main/original** location — more atmospheric than mall branches
- Cash or card accepted

## Nearby

West District, ~15 min by YouBike or NT$150 taxi from Miyahara.
