# LFP香料香水實驗室 — 台中綠光門市

DIY perfume blending workshop at the Green Ray (綠光計畫) location. LFP (La Fête Parfumée) is a Taiwanese fragrance brand with hands-on workshops where you blend your own scent from spices and fragrance bases.

Not on the original itinerary — a spontaneous discovery alongside Fantasy Green Ray.

## Notes (Apr 27)
Did the workshop while in the Green Ray area. Unexpected highlight of the day.

**Cost:** NT$1,550 (custom perfume blend) + NT$500 express production = **NT$2,050 total**
- Standard production takes ~30 days; express is 3–5 days
- Opted for express so it arrives before leaving Taiwan
