# 山東豆漿大王 (Shandong Soy Milk King)

> #eat · **✨ Worth It** · 💰 NT$80 · ✅ Done

Classic Taiwanese breakfast joint in Hualien. Known for soy milk (豆漿), shao bing you tiao (sesame flatbread with fried dough), and dan bing. A local staple.
