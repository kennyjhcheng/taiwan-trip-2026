# Taiping Suspension Bridge (太平吊橋) 🌉

Taiwan's longest and highest altitude sightseeing suspension bridge — 281 meters long at 1,000m elevation. Dramatic views of the valley and surrounding tea farms. A bucket-list infrastructure stop in the Chiayi foothills region.

## Getting There (without a car)

**2-leg public transport from Alishan — best as a transit stop.**

From Alishan:
1. Bus Alishan → Chiayi (~2h)
2. From Chiayi Station: take **Meishan-bound bus**
3. At Meishan: transfer to **Taiping-bound bus** → Taiping Suspension Bridge
4. ✅ **Discount:** Present your bus ticket at the bridge entrance for NT$80 admission (vs. NT$100)

**Best use case:** Visit Taiping Suspension Bridge on **May 1** as a stop en route from Alishan to Tainan — it's in the general direction of descent. Ask your accommodation to help route it.

## Notes

- ⚠️ Not practical as a day trip from Alishan — too much backtracking
- Best folded into the May 1 departure route (Alishan → bridge → Chiayi → Tainan)
