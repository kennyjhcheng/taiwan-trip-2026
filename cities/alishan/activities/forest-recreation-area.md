# Alishan National Forest Recreation Area 🌲

The main park — 2,200m altitude ancient red cypress forest. This is the core of the Alishan experience. Entrance ticket (NT$300) covers access to the entire recreation area including all walking trails, boardwalks, and the internal forest railway (Zhushan Line extra, see [Sacred Tree Station (神木車站)](sacred-tree-station.md)).

## What's Inside

The recreation area contains multiple interconnected walking loops:
- **Giant Tree Boardwalk** — 1.4 km elevated walkway through ancient cypress, 40–90 min
- **Sacred Tree Station & Shenmu Trail** — see [Sacred Tree Station (神木車站)](sacred-tree-station.md)
- **Three Generations Trees** — see [Three Generations Trees (三代木)](three-generations-trees.md)
- **Sister Ponds** — see [Sister Ponds (姊妹潭)](sister-ponds.md)
- **Zhaoping Station area** — cherry blossom trail
- **Ciyun Temple** — quiet forest temple on the loop

## Practical

- **Entrance fee:** NT$300 adult (buy at gate or online at [alishan.welcometw.com](https://alishan.welcometw.com))
- **Hours:** Open 24h — but services/trains run from ~06:00–17:00
- **Internal railway:** Shenmu Line runs every 15 min from 09:30–16:30 (10 min ride)
- **Crowd tip:** Tour groups concentrate between 11 AM–2 PM near the main entrance. Afternoon is quieter.
- **App:** Download the Alishan Forest Recreation Area map before going — offline useful

## Notes

One entrance ticket covers the whole day. Keep it — you may need it for re-entry.
