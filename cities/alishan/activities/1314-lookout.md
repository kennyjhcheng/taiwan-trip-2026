# 1314 Observation Deck 👀

A scenic lookout at 1,314m elevation on Haishu Mountain, with panoramic views over the Alishan foothills and tea gardens. "1314" is a play on the Chinese expression meaning "for life/forever" (一生一世), making it a romantic viewpoint.

## Getting There (without a car)

**Difficult from Alishan — better accessed from Chiayi.**

Public bus option from Chiayi:
- Take Chiayi County Bus 7315 (Chiayi–Ruifeng direction)
- Get off at Dipu stop
- Walk ~200m to the County Highway 166 km 37.8 intersection, follow "觀景樓" / "萬壽山茶園" signs
- Walk uphill 2 km to the lookout
- ⚠️ Only 2 buses each way per day — check schedule carefully

**From Alishan:** Not practical by public bus. Would require a taxi or hired driver (~NT$800–1,200 one way).
