# Sister Ponds (姊妹潭) 🌊

Two misty mountain ponds — Elder Sister Pond and Younger Sister Pond — set among ancient cypress and cedar trees at ~2,200m. Especially atmospheric in the late afternoon when mist rolls in. The loop trail circles both ponds and passes through dense forest.

## Trail

- **Loop distance:** ~400m around both ponds
- **Time:** ~45 min to 1h at a slow pace
- **Difficulty:** Easy, flat, paved
- **Best time:** Late afternoon (mist) or early morning

## Notes

- Covered by the NT$300 park admission — no extra charge
- Connects to the Ciyun Temple trail (~2.3 km beyond, adds 1h+)
- Plan this as the last stop of an afternoon forest walk: Giant Tree Boardwalk → Sacred Tree Station → Three Generations Trees → Sister Ponds (~2.5–3h total, leisurely)
- The legendary story of a Tsou maiden is posted at the entrance
