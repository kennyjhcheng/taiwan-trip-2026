# YUYUPAS Cultural Park (鄒族文化部落) 🎭

An indigenous Tsou cultural theme park at ~1,200m in the Shizhuo area, about 30–40 min from the main Alishan village. The name means "very rich" in Tsou language. Surrounded by tea and coffee plantations, the park offers guided Tsou cultural experiences, traditional performances, indigenous cuisine, and views of Alishan's mountain scenery.

## What's Inside

- **Tsou tribal cultural performances** — singing and dancing (schedule varies; check when booking)
- **Tea and coffee tasting** — from the surrounding Shizhuo tea plantations
- **Indigenous cuisine** — Tsou dishes using local mountain ingredients
- **Cultural exhibits** — Tsou history, traditional crafts, village life

## Getting There from Alishan (without a car)

**Doable by bus + call ahead.**

1. Take Chiayi County Bus from Alishan to **Shizhuo** (~26 min, every ~4h)
2. Call YUYUPAS at **05-256-2788** — they will come pick you up from Shizhuo (or walk ~50 min)
3. Return: arrange pickup back to Shizhuo with park staff, then bus back to Alishan

**Planning note:** With the 4-hour bus frequency, plan carefully. Leave Alishan early (first bus), spend 2–3h at the park, return on the next bus.

## Booking

- Book in advance at [Klook](https://www.klook.com/activity/59694-yuyupas-cultural-park-chiayi/) or call directly
- Tickets ~NT$350–500 depending on what's included
- Performances have fixed show times — confirm before visiting
