# Three Generations Trees (三代木) 🌲🌲🌲

A single ancient cypress that has fallen and sprouted a second generation from its trunk, which in turn has a third generation growing from it — three generations of trees, one on top of another. A striking visual symbol of Alishan's forest cycle.

## Location

A short walk from [Sacred Tree Station (神木車站)](sacred-tree-station.md) — follow trail signs within the park. No extra fee; covered by the NT$300 park admission.

## Notes

- Quick stop (~15 min) — combine with the Sacred Tree Station visit
- Directly on the route between Sacred Tree Station and Sister Ponds
- The symbolism (grandfather → parent → child trees) is explained on bilingual signage
