# Zhushan Sunrise Train (祝山觀日車)

> #activity · #sightseeing · **🔥 Must** · 💰 NT$100 · ⬜ Pending

Board at 4:30 AM. Summit at 2,451m. Sea of clouds fills the valleys at sunrise. On clear days: Jade Mountain (玉山, 3,952m) visible. One of the most spectacular things you can do in Taiwan. Buy tickets at station counter the afternoon before.
