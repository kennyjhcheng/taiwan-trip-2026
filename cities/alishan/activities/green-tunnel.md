# Green Tunnel (綠色隧道)

A beautiful canopy of trees arching over a quiet road in the Ruili area of the Alishan National Scenic Area — popular for cycling and walking. The tunnel of green is particularly striking in morning light.

## Getting There (without a car)

**Challenging without a car or guide.** Ruili is about 25 km from the main Alishan Forest Recreation Area.

Options:
- **Guided tour** — the most realistic option. Day tours from Chiayi often include the Green Tunnel en route to Alishan.
- **Taxi/car charter** from Alishan — ask your accommodation to arrange. A half-day charter costs roughly NT$1,500–2,500.
- **On arrival route:** Best visited on the way INTO Alishan (e.g., if taking a later bus from Chiayi that stops in the Ruili area), not as a standalone trip from Alishan.
