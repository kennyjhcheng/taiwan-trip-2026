## Week 2 Begins — Solo

## Morning — Beitou Hot Springs ♨️
- MRT Red Line → Xinbeitou Station (~30 min)
- **[[cities/taipei/activities/beitou-hot-springs]]** — Millennium Hot Spring (NT$40–80), open 5:30 AM
- Thermal Valley (地熱谷) — free scenic stop
- Beitou Hot Spring Museum — free, former Japanese bathhouse

## Afternoon — Elephant Mountain + Da'an
- MRT → Xiangshan Station → **[[cities/taipei/activities/elephant-mountain]]** — 20-min hike, golden hour Taipei 101 shot
- **Lunch: [[cities/taipei/activities/din-tai-fung]]** — original Xinyi location, queue by 11:30 AM
- Walk Da'an District: cafes, bookstores, Yongkang Street food density

## Evening — Ningxia Night Market
- **[[cities/taipei/activities/ningxia-night-market]]** — braised pork rice, oyster omelette, stinky tofu
