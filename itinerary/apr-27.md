# Apr 27 (Mon) — 🍦 Taichung — Full Day

**City:** [[cities/taichung/index]] | ← [[itinerary/apr-26]] | [[itinerary/apr-28]] →

---

## Morning — Central District

### 9:00 AM — Check in / Drop Luggage
- Head to **Backpacker 41 Hostel** (HostelWorld #95294-577100771)
- Check-in may not be until afternoon — leave luggage and head out
- [[logistics/accommodation#taichung-apr27]]

### 10:00 AM — Miyahara Ice Cream
- **[[cities/taichung/activities/miyahara-ice-cream]]** — Go early to beat the crowds
- Ornate Japanese-era building; choose your sundae and toppings at the counter
- Pick up pineapple cake gifts while you're there
- **~45 min**

### 11:00 AM — Animation Lane
- **[[cities/taichung/activities/animation-lane]]** — quick taxi north to Yizhong area
- Floor-to-ceiling anime murals; free, quick, great for photos
- Browse Yizhong Street snack stalls if anything looks good
- **~45 min**

---

## Midday — West District

### 12:00 PM — Chun Shui Tang (Lunch + Bubble Tea)
- **[[cities/taichung/activities/chun-shui-tang]]** — ~15 min taxi west
- Order the OG Pearl Milk Tea (珍珠奶茶) — you're at the birthplace
- Get the braised pork rice set for lunch
- **~1 hr**

### 1:30 PM — Capoo House + Fantasy Store Green Ray
- **[[cities/taichung/activities/capoo-house]]** — photos, merch, Capoo world
- **[[cities/taichung/activities/fantasy-store-green-ray]]** — vintage toys and anime collectibles
- Both are in the West / Central area; can be done back-to-back
- **~1–1.5 hrs combined**

### 3:00 PM — Minimal Michelin Ice Cream
- **[[cities/taichung/activities/minimal-michelin-ice-cream]]** — afternoon treat; confirm location on Google Maps
- **~20 min**

---

## Afternoon — Xitun District

### 3:30 PM — Taichung National Theatre
- **[[cities/taichung/activities/taichung-national-theatre]]** — ~20 min taxi west into Xitun
- Walk the free public floors and exterior; photograph the curved concrete "sound caves"
- **~45 min – 1 hr**

### 4:30 PM — Optional: Luce Memorial Chapel at Tunghai
- **[[cities/taichung/activities/luce-memorial-chapel]]** — ~15 min taxi from National Theatre
- I.M. Pei masterpiece; peaceful campus; free
- Skip if tired — head straight to Fengchia to rest and eat early
- **~30 min**

### Optional Detour: Rainbow Village
- **[[cities/taichung/activities/rainbow-village]]** — works if routing south between Miyahara and Chun Shui Tang
- 20–30 min visit, free

---

## Evening — Fengchia Night Market

### 5:30 PM — Night School Braised Pork Rice
- **[[cities/taichung/activities/night-school-braised-pork-rice]]** — proper dinner before the market
- Fill up first; you'll graze at the market too
- **~30 min**

### 6:30 PM – 9:30 PM — Fengchia Night Market
- **[[cities/taichung/activities/fengchia-night-market]]** — one of Taiwan's biggest; Monday = manageable crowds
- Grilled corn · stinky tofu · sausage with sticky rice · oyster vermicelli
- Explore the side alleys for the more local stalls
- **~2.5–3 hrs**
