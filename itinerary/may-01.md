# May 1 (Fri) — 🏛️ Tainan — Old City

**City:** [Tainan](../cities/tainan/index.md) | ← [Apr 30](apr-30.md) | [May 02](may-02.md) →

---

> Tainan is Taiwan's oldest city and food capital. Slow down here — the point is wandering, eating, and stumbling into temples.

---

## Morning

- Slow start · local breakfast near your accommodation
- **[Anping Old Street](../cities/tainan/activities/anping-old-street.md)** (安平老街) — Dutch-era fort district, 17th century · free to wander
- **Anping Fort (安平古堡)** — NT$70

## Midday

- **[Coffin Bread (棺材板)](../cities/tainan/activities/coffin-bread.md)** (棺材板) or **[Milkfish Congee (虱目魚粥)](../cities/tainan/activities/milkfish-congee.md)** — iconic Tainan lunches
- Shennong Street for coffee and old shophouses

## Afternoon

- **[Hayashi Department Store](../cities/tainan/activities/hayashi-department-store.md)** (林百貨) — 1930s Japanese department store, beautifully restored
- **Tainan Confucius Temple** (孔廟) — Taiwan's oldest, serene courtyard
- More temple-hopping: Sacrificial Rites Martial Temple (祀典武廟), Koxinga Shrine (延平郡王祠)

## Evening

- **[Flower Night Market](../cities/tainan/activities/flower-night-market.md)** or **[Garden Night Market (花園夜市)](../cities/tainan/activities/garden-night-market.md)** — check which is open Friday
- **Tainan shaved ice** — Qiaowei Mango Ice or any local shop
