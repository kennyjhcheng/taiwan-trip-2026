### 11:10 AM — Nine Frog Stack
- **[Nine Frog Stack (九蛙疊像)](../cities/sun-moon-lake/activities/nine-frog-stack.md)** · NT$0 · ~15 min
- Famous lakeside sculpture of 9 stacked stone frogs at the water's edge — lake level determines how many are visible

### 11:30 AM — Ride to Ita Thao

## 1:00 PM — Continue Loop: Xuanzang Temple + Ci'en Pagoda

- **[Xuanzang Temple (玄奘寺)](../cities/sun-moon-lake/activities/xuanzang-temple.md)** · NT$0 · ~20 min · Buddhist temple housing a relic of the monk Xuanzang (the real-life inspiration for Journey to the West)
- **[Ci'en Pagoda (慈恩塔)](../cities/sun-moon-lake/activities/cien-pagoda.md)** · NT$0 · ~45 min · lock bike at trailhead, hike up for the best full-lake panorama
- Both are on the east shore — do back to back
