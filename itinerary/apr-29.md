# Apr 29 (Wed) — 🌅 SML Sunrise → Alishan Day Trip → Chiayi

**City:** [Alishan](../cities/alishan/index.md) | ← [Apr 28](apr-28.md) | [Apr 30](apr-30.md) →

---

## 5:30 AM — Sunrise at Shuishe Pier 🔥
- **[Sunrise at Shuishe Pier](../cities/sun-moon-lake/activities/sunrise-shuishe-pier.md)** — free · unmissable
- Lake in morning mist — one of the most beautiful moments in Taiwan
- Walk 5 min from hotel to the pier; bring a jacket

---

## Morning — Check Out & Bus to Alishan

### 7:00 AM — Breakfast & Check Out
- Quick breakfast near Shuishe, check out and store/bring luggage

### 7:45 AM — Arrive at Bus Stop ⚠️ Must be here 15 min early
- 📍 **No. 163, Zhongshan Rd, Yuchi Township, Nantou County**
- Short walk or taxi from hotel — confirm location the night before

- **台灣好行 Route 6739 · 日月潭阿里山線 (去程)**
- Carrier: 員林客運 (Yuanlin Bus) · Departs → 阿里山轉運站
- Order: O6979252790 · Payment: PO-5819570486
- 📱 Scan QR code **on boarding AND alighting** · max brightness · iPhone keep away from Apple Pay
- Voucher: https://voucher.fontour.com/p/M24eLd-O6979252790

## ~10:30 AM — Arrive Alishan (阿里山轉運站)

- Drop luggage at a coin locker at the transit hub if available, or carry a daypack
- Grab a jacket — it's ~15°C cooler than SML

---

## Midday — Lunch in the Village

- **[Mountain Vegetable Set Meal](../cities/alishan/activities/mountain-vegetable-meal.md)** — NT$300
- Wild ferns, bamboo shoots, local greens in any village restaurant near the transit hub

---

## Afternoon — Alishan Forest Recreation Area 🌲

> **Buy park entry at the gate: NT$300/person**

### ~12:30 PM — Enter the Park
- **[Alishan National Forest Recreation Area](../cities/alishan/activities/forest-recreation-area.md)** — NT$300

### ~1:00 PM — Giant Tree Boardwalk
- **[Giant Tree Trail (巨木群棧道)](../cities/alishan/activities/giant-tree-trail.md)** — 1.4km elevated walkway through ancient cypress, 40–90 min
- Walk the boardwalk on foot — far more atmospheric than the internal railway

### ~2:30 PM — Sacred Tree Station
- **[Sacred Tree Station (神木車站)](../cities/alishan/activities/sacred-tree-station.md)** — free (included in park)
- Site of the original 2,700-year-old fallen cypress

### ~3:00 PM — Three Generations Trees
- **[Three Generations Trees (三代木)](../cities/alishan/activities/three-generations-trees.md)** — free · 5 min from Sacred Tree Station

### ~3:30 PM — Sister Ponds (姊妹潭)
- **[Sister Ponds (姊妹潭)](../cities/alishan/activities/sister-ponds.md)** — free · 400m loop · 45 min

---

## ~4:30 PM — Bus to Chiayi

- Board any Alishan → Chiayi bus at 阿里山轉運站
- Journey: ~2h · NT$240 cash or NT$211 EasyCard (Chiayi County Bus 7322)
- ⚠️ Last buses around 5:10 PM — don't miss it

---

## ~6:30 PM — Arrive Chiayi

- Check in → [Chiayi](../logistics/accommodation.md#chiayi)
- **[Chiayi Turkey Rice (嘉義火雞肉飯)](../cities/other/activities/chiayi-turkey-rice.md)** — NT$50–80 · the dish Chiayi is most famous for
- Explore Chenghuang Temple night area or the Wenhua Road night market

**Stay:** [Chiayi](../logistics/accommodation.md#chiayi)
