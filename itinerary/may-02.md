## Breakfast
- [Milkfish Congee (虱目魚粥)](../cities/tainan/activities/milkfish-congee.md) or another Tainan morning institution

## Morning — Anping District
- **[Fort Zeelandia (安平古堡)](../cities/tainan/activities/fort-zeelandia.md)** — 17th-century Dutch colonial fort
- **[Anping Old Street](../cities/tainan/activities/anping-old-street.md)** — shrimp rolls, tofu pudding (豆花), waterfront
- **[Anping Tree House](../cities/tainan/activities/anping-tree-house.md)** — century-old banyan-wrapped warehouse

## Lunch
- **[Coffin Bread (棺材板)](../cities/tainan/activities/coffin-bread.md)** — near Chihkan Tower. Tainan invented this.

## Afternoon — Temples + History
- **[Confucius Temple (孔廟)](../cities/tainan/activities/confucius-temple.md)** — Taiwan's first, built 1665
- **[Chihkan Tower (赤崁樓)](../cities/tainan/activities/chihkan-tower.md)** — Dutch/Ming administrative center
- **[Temple Crawl](../cities/tainan/activities/temple-crawl.md)** — wander freely; 300+ temples

## Evening — Garden Night Market 🎉
- **[Garden Night Market (花園夜市)](../cities/tainan/activities/garden-night-market.md)** — open Sat ✅ — shrimp rolls, sausage-wrapped rice, peanut soup
