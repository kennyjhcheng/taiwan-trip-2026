## Taipei MRT Tips

- EasyCard (悠遊卡) — reloadable, works on MRT, buses, 7-Eleven, FamilyMart
- Airport Express: separate card or cash purchase
- Key lines:
  - **Red Line** — airport, Beitou, Xiangshan (Elephant Mountain), Danshui
  - **Brown Line** — Taipei Zoo → Maokong Gondola (MRT endpoint)
  - **Blue Line** — Taipei Main Station hub
